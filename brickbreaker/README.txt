Welcome to BrickBreaker 
1.Press"開始"to start the game.
2.Whenever you press"重設" game will stop and reset ,back to 1. to start the game.
3.RadioButton provide different speed of ball try your limit.
4.Dont let the ball and clean all the brick to win the game! 

Bonus:
按下"不死"可以使地板變能反彈,無法想像輸的樣子. 
遊戲進行中可以欣賞圖片(隨磚塊擊破數上升可以看到完整圖片)!
